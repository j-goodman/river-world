River World is a free-to-play tabletop RPG. It's played with three regular six-sided dice, and is designed to be light on mechanics to make room for storytelling.

Your game can happen on a river, an ocean, a desert, or any other kind of world you’re interested in building with your group. It can be about heroes and monsters, romance, politics, business, adventure, or whatever. All the information you need to play is in the River World PDF.
