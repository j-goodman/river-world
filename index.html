River World is a free-to-play tabletop RPG. It's played with three regular six-sided dice, and is designed to be light on mechanics to make room for storytelling.

Your game can take place in whatever kind of world you’re interested in building with your group. All the information you need to play is in the River World PDF.

This PDF is designed to be printed as a booklet and stapled together. To print it correctly, make sure your printer settings are set to print double-sided and to flip on long edge. The booklet will use 21 sheets of paper. Once it's printed, you can fold it in half and bind it with three staples in the center.
